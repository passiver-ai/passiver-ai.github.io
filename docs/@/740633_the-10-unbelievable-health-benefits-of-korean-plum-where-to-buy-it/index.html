Cooking with Fresh Korean Plums - Recipes Revealed!

Fresh Korean plums are a versatile ingredient that can be used in various dishes to add a unique tangy flavor. Here are some delicious recipes that showcase the incredible taste of fresh Korean plums:

  1. Plum Glazed Chicken: Marinate chicken breasts in a mixture of soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, and pureed fresh plums. Grill or bake the chicken until cooked through and brush with additional plum glaze while cooking for an extra burst of flavor.

  2. Korean Plum Salad: Toss together mixed greens, sliced cucumber, cherry tomatoes, and thinly sliced fresh plums. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice dressing for a refreshing summer salad.

  3. Plum Salsa: Combine diced fresh plums with chopped red onion, cilantro leaves, lime juice, salt, and pepper. Serve as a topping for grilled fish or tacos for an explosion of flavors.

  4. Plum Smoothie: Blend together frozen banana slices, almond milk (or any other plant-based milk), spinach leaves (for added nutrition), Greek yogurt (for creaminess), and a few slices of fresh plum for sweetness.

These recipes are just the tip of the iceberg when it comes to incorporating fresh Korean plums into your meals. Get creative in the kitchen and experiment with different combinations to find your own favorite dishes!

Making Delicious Beverages using Dried Plum Extracts

If you prefer a quicker and more convenient way to enjoy the health benefits of Korean plum tea, consider using dried plum extracts. Here are some delicious beverages that can be easily made using dried plum extracts:

  1. Iced Plum Tea: Mix a spoonful of dried plum extract with cold water and ice cubes for a refreshing summer drink. You can also add a squeeze of lemon juice or honey for added flavor.

  2. Plum Infused Water: Add a few drops of dried plum extract to your regular drinking water for a subtle hint of flavor and an antioxidant boost.

  3. Plum Mojito Mocktail: Muddle fresh mint leaves, lime wedges, and a spoonful of dried plum extract in a glass. Top it off with sparkling water and crushed ice for an alcohol-free twist on the classic mojito.

  4. Plum Smoothie Bowl: Blend frozen mixed berries, almond milk (or any other plant-based milk), Greek yogurt, spinach leaves (for added nutrition), and a spoonful of dried plum extract until smooth. Pour into bowls and top with your favorite toppings like granola, sliced fruits, or chia seeds.

These delightful beverage options will not only quench your thirst but also provide you with the numerous health benefits that Korean plums offer.
